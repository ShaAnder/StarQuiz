Episode IV

Team 5 Does The Hackathon


The theme, Star Wars, the mission, to create a fun and engaging quiz based app, our team sets out to create an interactive map of episodes IV, V and VI documenting Luke Skywalkers harrowing adventures through space with his friends.

What will we find, interesting trivia? Fun facts? Detailed plot points of the movies? Who only knows but it's in your hands to search all of our planets, gather your knowledge and overcome the final challenge!

Test your love and knowledge of starwars in this interactive app created for the may 22nd Code Institute hackathon!

Created by: ShaAnder, Joha Wilson, Teo_alina, Paul Hughes, Beth Cottell, Cecile Gaudron and Sam Atkinson
